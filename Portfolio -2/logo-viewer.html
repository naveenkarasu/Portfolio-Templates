Logo Comparison: GLB vs PNG

Option 1: GLB (3D Model)

Pros: Interactive, rotatable, scalable, modern
Cons: Larger file size, requires Three.js to render
Best for: Hero section with 3D background

Option 2: PNG (Static Image)

Pros: Fast loading, works everywhere, simple
Cons: Static, can't interact or animate
Best for: Navbar, favicon, footer
